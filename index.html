About Me

Amen Allah Lamrani is a Tunisian Audio Engineer and Sound Designer focused on cinematic sound creation. His work combines technical precision with emotional storytelling, building depth, tension, and atmosphere in every project.

Specializing in sound editing, Foley, production sound, and mixing, he crafts immersive soundscapes that elevate narrative impact across drama, sci-fi, horror, and war films.

HORROR 2023
WESTERN

Rolling Dice

Boom Operator • Sound Editor

A gritty western following two outlaws on the run from their violent past. As boom operator, I captured authentic location sound in harsh desert conditions. In post-production, I edited dialogue and ambient tracks to preserve the raw, windswept atmosphere of the frontier.

RADIO

La Pièce

Writer • Sound Designer • Mixer

An immersive radio fiction set during the Tunisian revolution, exploring themes of confinement and political awakening. I wrote the script, designed the complete soundscape using spatial audio techniques, and mixed the final piece to create a visceral listening experience that transports audiences into the heart of the uprising.

WAR

The Last Letter

Boom Operator • Sound Editor

A poignant war drama about a soldier torn between duty and family. I operated boom on location, capturing clean dialogue amid challenging combat sequences. During editing, I shaped the sonic contrast between the chaos of battle and the intimate quiet of personal moments, emphasizing the emotional weight of sacrifice.

DRAMA

Rotten Salt

Sound Engineer • Mixer

An intimate character study exploring grief, denial, and the slow process of acceptance. I engineered production sound and mixed the final audio, focusing on subtle environmental details and silence to reflect the protagonist's emotional isolation and gradual healing.

SCI-FI

Salvation

Boom Operator • Sound Editor

A thought-provoking sci-fi drama about a humanoid robot questioning its existence and purpose. As boom operator, I captured nuanced performances in sterile, futuristic environments. In post, I edited mechanical and synthetic sound elements to create an otherworldly sonic identity that blurs the line between human and machine.

DRAMA

The Other Side

Sound Engineer • Mixer

A family drama centered on a young artist's struggle for identity and acceptance. I engineered location sound and mixed the final audio, emphasizing the tension between domestic spaces and creative freedom through careful balance of dialogue clarity and environmental texture.
